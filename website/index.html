Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Auto-Scheduler

Constraint-satisfaction solver that slots tasks by priority, deadline urgency, and available time. Preview before applying.

Google Calendar Sync

Two-way sync with Google Calendar. Per-event colors, multi-slot scheduling, and automatic worklog creation.

Jira Integration

Pull assigned issues, filter by status, group by epic. Fuzzy search, multi-select, and JQL filter support.

GitHub PR Tracking

Track PRs requiring your review. Schedule review time directly on your calendar with dedicated PR colors.

Deadline Risk Badges

Real-time risk assessment: ON_TRACK, AT_RISK, URGENT, OVERDUE. Never miss a deadline again.

Desktop Native

Built with Tauri and Rust. Lightweight, fast, and private — your data stays on your machine.

How it Works

Three steps to a
stress-free week.

01

Connect

Link your Jira, Google Calendar, and GitHub accounts. Mira securely stores tokens in your system keyring.

02

Pull

Mira fetches your assigned tasks and open PRs. Filter by status, group by epic, or use custom JQL queries.

03

Schedule

Hit Auto-Schedule and Mira slots everything into your calendar around existing events. Review the preview, then apply.
